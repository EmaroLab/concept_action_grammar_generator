19  /* ##################################################################################
20  ######################### CONSTANTS UTILITY CLASS ################################
21  used to parse tags for source file. Particularly, specifications are:
22  - [@~] impose to push to the leafs of the composed tree a tag equal to their
23  term value (it cannot be controlled) (it should be implemented by ActionTagBase.parse)
24  - [@T] impose to push to the leafs of the composed tree the T tag
25  (can be controlled by "!") (implemented on ExpressionNode.composeTree)
26  - [@?] impose to push to the leafs of the composed tree a tag equal to the sub
27  rule definition name relate to this tag (can be controlled by "!")
28  (implemented on ExpressionNode.composeTree)
29  - [@!T] impose to stop pushing to the leafs of the composed tree the tag T
30  (it can be used to control "?") (implemented on ExpressionNode.composeTree)
31  - [@!!] impose to stop pushing all the tag to the children sub rules of the
32  composed tree. (it cannot be controlled) (implemented on ExpressionNode.composeTree)
33  - [@!~] impose to stop pushing the tag equal to the term value to the children
34  sub rules of the composed tree. (it cannot be controlled) (implemented on ExpressionNode.composeTree)
35  - [@?~] it is a short cut that stands for [@?;@:]. */
