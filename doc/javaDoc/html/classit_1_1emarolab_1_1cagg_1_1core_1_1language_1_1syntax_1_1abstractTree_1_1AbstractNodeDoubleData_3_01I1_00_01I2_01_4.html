This method is used to format the data into the GUI. It is invoked when the GUI refreshes the node data visualisation by calling: it.emarolab.cagg.core.language.syntax.abstractTree.AbstractDataFactory.DataFactoryVisualizer#showContents(AbstractNodeData, JPanel) or it.emarolab.cagg.core.language.syntax.abstractTree.AbstractDataFactory.DataFactoryVisualizer#showContents(AbstractNodeDoubleData, JPanel).
This method should use getVisualizer() in order to add to the

contentPanel

the visualisation of the data (through getInstance() and getInstance2()) and the information (getInfo1() and getInfo2()). The implementation automatically care about visualising genericInfo.
