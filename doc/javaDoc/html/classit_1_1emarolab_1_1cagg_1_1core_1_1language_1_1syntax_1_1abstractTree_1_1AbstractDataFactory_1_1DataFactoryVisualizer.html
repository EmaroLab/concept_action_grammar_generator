Particularly, it is used in: it.emarolab.cagg.core.language.syntax.abstractTree.AbstractNodeData, it.emarolab.cagg.core.language.syntax.abstractTree.AbstractNodeDoubleData and their extending classes.
Finally, to get a new instance of this object you should use: AbstractDataFactory#getNewVisualizer()
